Generating Questions
Instructions

Welcome to our application! Follow these steps to generate question from content:

Step 1:   Load/Type the text in the provided text editor (min 1000 characters). Step 2:   Click on the generate questions button available at the bottom of the left
to load the questions. Step 3:   Wait for the questions to be loaded. Step 4:   Modify the generated question and options and other data. Step 5:   Provide feedback on the generated questions.
